## Recommended Version 1.1 Scope

### 1. Resume Parsing Improvements

Goal: Improve candidate onboarding and application quality.

Scope:
- Better PDF/DOCX extraction.
- Structured fields: skills, employers, titles, education, years of experience.
- Candidate review/confirm step.
- Parsing confidence indicators.

Effort: 2-3 weeks  
Business impact: High

### 2. AI Matching

Goal: Increase application quality and employer activation.

Scope:
- Match score between candidate profile and job.
- Explainable match factors.
- Candidate suggestions: roles to apply for.
- Employer suggestions: strong-fit applicants.

Effort: 4-6 weeks  
Business impact: Very high

### 3. Employer Branding

Goal: Convert more employers into paying customers.

Scope:
- Rich company profile pages.
- Impact metrics and mission content.
- Photos/logos/media.
- Featured jobs.
- Review highlights.

Effort: 2-4 weeks  
Business impact: High

### 4. Talent Intelligence

Goal: Differentiate from generic job boards.

Scope:
- Sector salary insights.
- Talent supply by role/location.
- Application conversion benchmarks.
- Hiring funnel dashboard.

Effort: 5-8 weeks  
Business impact: Very high

## Deferred to Version 1.2

- ATS integrations.
- Interview scheduling.
- Recruiter collaboration.
- LinkedIn import through compliant user-authorized flows.

## Deferred to Version 2.0

- Executive Search CRM.
- Advanced referral marketplace.
- Enterprise account hierarchy.
- Custom client reporting.

## Version 1.1 Success Metrics

- Employer activation rate increases by 20%.
- Candidate activation rate increases by 20%.
- Application-to-job ratio improves by 25%.
- CV parsing failure rate drops below 2%.
- At least 30% of applications include structured skills data.
