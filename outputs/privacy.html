Privacy Policy

This beta privacy notice is provided for launch readiness and requires legal review before public production.

Information collected

We collect account details, company profiles, job posts, applications, uploaded files, reviews, salary signals, support tickets, and operational analytics needed to run the platform.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, display jobs and profiles, route applications, provide support, improve reliability, and operate analytics dashboards.

Private data

Candidate private information, uploaded CVs, and account details should not be exposed publicly. Salary aggregates and anonymous reviews are designed to avoid exposing private user identity.

Processors

Production may use Supabase, Vercel, Resend or another email provider, OpenAI, Sentry, and optional analytics tools when configured.

User choices

Users can contact support for account questions, correction requests, or deletion requests during beta.
