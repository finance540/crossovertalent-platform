FAQ

Commercial launch questions.

Clear expectations for employers, candidates, and beta partners.

Who is CrossOver Talent for?

Employers and candidates working across climate, impact investment, public healthcare, clean energy, education, agriculture, water, foundations, CSR, ESG consulting, and adjacent public-good markets.

Can employers manage applications?

Yes. Employers can review applications and move candidates through Applied, Shortlisted, Interview, Offered, Rejected, and Hired.

Can candidates track application status?

Yes. Candidate dashboards show saved jobs, applied jobs, resume tools, preferences, and status history.

Are pricing and payments live?

No. Pricing is placeholder-only for Version 1.0. Production payments should wait for the subscription entitlement layer.

Does AI always use OpenAI?

Live AI works when configured. If the API key is missing or unavailable, the app shows safe fallback drafts or messages instead of breaking.

How do I get support?

Use the Support button for feedback, bug reports, feature requests, or launch support.
