CTcrossover talent

Help Center

Employers

Create an employer workspace, verify your email, complete your company profile, upload a logo, post a job, and manage candidates from Applications.

  • Use the JD assistant after adding title, skills, experience, KPIs, and KRAs.
  • Close a job when it should stop accepting applications.
  • Update candidate status promptly so candidates can track progress.

Candidates

Browse jobs before signup. Create an account to save jobs, apply, upload your CV, revise your resume, and track applications.

  • Use text-based PDF, DOCX, or TXT files for best CV parsing.
  • Withdraw applications from your dashboard unless the application is already Offered or Hired.
  • Keep preferences current for better guidance.

Admins

Use the admin dashboard to monitor operations, moderate reviews/jobs, manage users, and triage feedback tickets.

  • Review support tickets daily during beta.
  • Escalate signup, email verification, application, upload, and security issues immediately.
  • Use health/readiness endpoints before launch checks.
