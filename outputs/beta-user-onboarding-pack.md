# Crossover Talent Beta User Onboarding Pack

Date: July 3, 2026  
Environment: Private Beta / Staging Preview

## Welcome

You are joining a controlled Private Beta for Crossover Talent - Impact Career Intelligence. The goal is to test real workflows for impact-sector hiring before public production launch.

Please use the beta environment only for testing. Do not upload highly sensitive personal or confidential business documents during beta.

## Employer Onboarding Instructions

1. Open the beta link shared by the Crossover Talent team.
2. Create an employer account.
3. Complete the email verification step using the beta instructions provided.
4. Log in to the employer dashboard.
5. Create or update your company profile:
   - Company name
   - Website
   - Sector
   - Location
   - Description
   - Logo
6. Post at least one job.
7. Edit the job after posting.
8. Publish the job.
9. Confirm the job appears on the public job board.
10. Review incoming applications.
11. Change at least one candidate status.

Recommended employer test:

- Create 1 company profile.
- Upload or replace 1 logo.
- Post 2 jobs.
- Publish 1 job and keep 1 as draft/unpublished.
- Review at least 1 application.

## Candidate Onboarding Instructions

1. Open the beta link shared by the Crossover Talent team.
2. Create a candidate account.
3. Complete the email verification step using the beta instructions provided.
4. Log in to the candidate dashboard.
5. Create or update your profile.
6. Add your LinkedIn profile link.
7. Upload a CV or resume file.
8. Browse jobs.
9. Use search and filters.
10. Save at least one job.
11. Apply to at least one job.
12. Track your application status from the candidate dashboard.
13. Test withdrawing an application if you are comfortable doing so.

Recommended candidate test:

- Save 2 jobs.
- Apply to 1 job.
- Submit 1 company review.
- Submit 1 salary signal.
- Report any confusing or broken workflow.

## Known Limitations

This beta is not public production.

Known beta limitations:

- AI may run in fallback-only mode if live OpenAI access is not configured.
- Email verification may use a staging workaround instead of real inbox delivery.
- Some seeded/demo-like staging records may exist for QA purposes.
- Production custom domain and production infrastructure are not yet active.
- The team may reset staging data during beta if required for testing.

## Feedback Form Questions

Recommended feedback form:

1. Your name
2. Your email
3. Your role: employer, candidate, internal tester, admin
4. Which page or workflow were you testing?
5. What were you trying to do?
6. What happened?
7. What did you expect to happen?
8. How severe is the issue?
   - Blocker
   - Serious issue
   - Minor issue
   - Suggestion
9. What browser and device were you using?
10. Can we contact you for follow-up?
11. Screenshot or screen recording upload

## Bug Reporting Instructions

When reporting a bug, include:

- The beta URL you were using.
- Your role: employer, candidate, admin, or tester.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Expected result.
- Actual result.
- Screenshot or recording, if available.
- Browser and device.
- Whether the issue blocks your workflow.

Bug priority guide:

| Priority | Meaning |
|---|---|
| P0 | The app is unavailable, data is exposed, or core workflows are completely blocked. |
| P1 | A critical employer, candidate, or admin workflow is broken with no practical workaround. |
| P2 | Important issue with a workaround. |
| P3 | Polish, copy, layout, or enhancement request. |
