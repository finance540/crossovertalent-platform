# Beta Success Dashboard

Date: July 3, 2026

## Objective

Measure whether CrossOver Talent is ready to expand beyond controlled public beta after 30 days.

## Core Success Metrics

| Metric | Definition | 30-Day Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Employer activation rate | Employers with at least one job / total employers | 50%+ |
| Candidate activation rate | Candidates with at least one application / total candidates | 20%+ |
| Time to first job post | Employer signup to first job posted | Median under 48h |
| Time to first application | Candidate signup to first application | Median under 72h |
| Applications per job | Applications / active jobs | 3+ average |
| Employer retention | Employers active in week 4 after signup | 40%+ |
| Candidate retention | Candidates active in week 4 after signup | 25%+ |
| AI adoption | AI JD/CV events / active users | 20%+ |
| Daily active users | Unique users with product events in 24h | Trending upward |
| Weekly active users | Unique users with product events in 7 days | Trending upward |
| Monthly active users | Unique users with product events in 30 days | 500+ by day 30 |
| Net Promoter Score | Placeholder survey score | Baseline only |

## Reliability Success Criteria

| Metric | Target |
|---|---:|
| Email success rate | 95%+ |
| Upload failure rate | Below 5% |
| AI fallback/error rate | Below 20% unless fallback-only mode is intentional |
| Average API latency | Below 1,000 ms |
| P0 incidents | 0 |
| Open P1 bugs at day 30 | 0 |

## Commercial Success Criteria

| Metric | Target |
|---|---:|
| Employers onboarded | 100 invited, 50+ activated |
| Candidates onboarded | 5,000 invited, 1,000+ activated |
| Jobs posted | 100+ |
| Applications submitted | 300+ |
| Employer feedback interviews | 10+ |
| Candidate feedback responses | 100+ |

## Expansion Gate

Expand beyond controlled public beta only if:
- No open P0/P1 issues.
- Employer activation is above 50%.
- Email success is above 95%.
- Support volume is manageable with current operations.
- At least 10 employers confirm the platform is useful enough to keep using.

## NPS Placeholder

Survey prompt:
> How likely are you to recommend CrossOver Talent to a colleague working in climate, public health, impact investing, or sustainability?

Scale: 0-10

Follow-up:
- What was most useful?
- What nearly blocked you?
- What should we improve first?
